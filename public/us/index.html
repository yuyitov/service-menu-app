FAQ

Straight answers

No inflated promises — here's what HMU Link does and doesn't do.

Is it just a QR code?

No. The QR code is only one way to share your page. What you get is a full service page with your services, prices, promotions, hours, location, and contact buttons — plus a shareable link and the QR code.

Do I need an app?

No. Neither you nor your clients download anything. Your page works in any browser, on any phone or computer.

Can I use it in my Instagram bio?

Yes. Your link works perfectly as your Instagram link-in-bio, and you can share it in stories or reply to DMs with it.

Can I use it with WhatsApp or texts?

Yes. Send your link in any chat instead of typing out your price list, and your page includes a WhatsApp button so clients can message you directly.

Can I change my prices later?

Yes. Your package includes one free revision. After that, additional changes are billed separately as extra revisions.

Does it include booking?

Booking and contact happen through WhatsApp, Instagram, Google Maps, or your external booking link (for example, your existing scheduling tool). There's no built-in booking system yet.

Can I pick my own colors?

You choose from 6 curated visual styles designed to look professional for any business. There's no free-form color picking — that's how we guarantee your page always looks good.

Does it work for barbershops?

Yes. Styles like Charcoal Clean and Black Gold were designed with barbershops and men's grooming in mind. Check out the North Barber example.

Does it work for businesses other than salons and spas?

Yes. It works for any local service business: nails, lashes, massage, tours, fitness studios, classes, coaches, pet grooming, tattoo studios, and independent professionals.

When can I buy it?

The product is in preparation. The examples are already public and real; checkout will be enabled in an upcoming phase. The pricing shown is tentative launch pricing.
